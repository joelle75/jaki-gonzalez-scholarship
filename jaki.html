Jaki Gonzalez Spirit of Excellence Scholarship

A Spirit of Excellence

Jaki graduated Valedictorian of her class and earned a full-tuition scholarship to Princeton University, a testament to her brilliance and work ethic. She believed deeply in the power of education to create change.

A Leader Beyond the Classroom

Jaki embraced every opportunity to lead and uplift others, including on the basketball court. Her dedication, teamwork, and determination made her a standout both as an athlete and as a friend and role model.

In Her Own Words

At her high school graduation, Jaki delivered a heartfelt and inspiring speech that emphasized doing your best with what you have, and trusting your own timing. Her message resonated deeply and reflected the wisdom and grace with which she lived.

A Beacon of Belonging

Jaki Gonzalez was a brilliant and dedicated student, deeply engaged in the Princeton community through the Freshman Scholars Institute and the Scholars Institute Fellows Program. As a virtual college adviser, she warmly welcomed new students and made a lasting impact. Her legacy of compassion and leadership continues to inspire those who knew her.

A Legacy of Excellence

This scholarship is one of many ways we carry forward Jaki’s light. Her legacy lives on in the lives of every student we support—each one touched by her story of resilience and excellence.
