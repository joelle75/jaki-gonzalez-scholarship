Our Mission

The Jaki Gonzalez Spirit of Excellence Scholarship celebrates the memory of Jaki Gonzalez '21 by supporting outstanding students who exemplify excellence, resilience, and a commitment to service. Through this fund, we aim to honor her legacy and empower future changemakers.
