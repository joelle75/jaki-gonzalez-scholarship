Our Mission

The Jaki Gonzalez Spirit of Excellence Scholarship honors the life and legacy of Jaki Gonzalez ’21 by supporting exceptional students at Harvard High School in Harvard, Illinois—students who, like Jaki, embody excellence, resilience, and a deep commitment to service. This scholarship not only celebrates who Jaki was, but also invests in the next generation of changemakers who carry forward her spirit and determination.
