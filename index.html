Our Mission

The Jaki Gonzalez Spirit of Excellence Scholarship honors the life and legacy of Jaki Gonzalez ’21, who rose from humble beginnings to graduate Valedictorian of Harvard High School’s 100th class and earn a full-tuition scholarship to Princeton University. Jaki’s story is one of resilience, brilliance, and unwavering dedication to learning. This scholarship celebrates her remarkable journey and supports exceptional seniors from Harvard High School who, like Jaki, embody excellence and determination. We invite you to donate and help carry her spirit forward.
